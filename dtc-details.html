DTC (Dubai Taxi Corporation)

DTC (Dubai Taxi Corporation) • DTC (Dubai Taxi Corporation) is an infotainment application installed in the Android POS device.
• The main screen displayed the images and video content, in addition there were trip details for the driver, banners, weather forecast, news update, technology or information updates section and other services.
• The purpose of these applications was to be installed in the taxis for the customers to engage them with the non-stop entertainment.
• These Android POS devices were installed in more than 800 taxis in Dubai.
• All the data shown on the screen was dynamic, fetched from the services and displayed on the Android POS devices.
• There were different APIs for each and every content, the APIs were consumed in an application, the response fetched from the APIs were manipulated and shown on the Android POS devices.
The description of the content and services are mentioned below
• Banners:
The top part contains the banners where the latest offers & promotions and ads were shown dynamically
• Videos:
Dubai government ‘Smart Teller Machine’ videos which helps customers by -showing the video to skip the long lines at the counter and use the machine for the services.
